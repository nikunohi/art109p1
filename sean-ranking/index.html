SJSU North Garage

The SJSU North Garage is a six story parking garage with parking available on floors 02-06. Students may park on floors 04-06, and on half of floor 03, which is shared with faculty. The structure is made almost completely out of concrete, with pillars that encroach on many of the parking spaces. The aisles can be narrow for cars, especially on the roof floor. At noon on a typical academic day, the North Garage, which spans about a city block, is almost always at full capacity.

Use the landscape page-scroll bar to reveal the entire garage. Rankings may be scattered.
